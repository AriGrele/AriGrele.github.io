Rapid Image Annotator

A tool for rappidly associating spatial and textual information with large numbers of images.

Current capabilities:

  • Toolsets geared for extremly rapid image annotation. Users report speeds of up to ~1,000 images annotated per hour for simple tasks and ~200 image per hour for complex tasks
  • Spatial annotation of bounding boxes, bounding circles, lines, and points
  • Text annotation of entire image or individual spatial markers
  • Complete customizability of keyboard inputs
  • Currently only available for Windows 10-11

Things I developed this software for:

  • Annotation of 510,000 insect bounding boxes for computer vision training
  • Labeling of 8,600 milkweed flowers with age classifications
  • Measuring of 17,500 scale bars for standardizing herbivory analyses
